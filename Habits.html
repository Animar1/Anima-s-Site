We all have habits with daily routines in our lives, but people with ocd can have more add ons of daily routines. Walking, thinking on how to say something, eating, organizing clothes showering are how ocd people are affected. With thinking they have to do things a certain way. Their thoughts are what leads them to react the way they react. The more they are used to those routines the more it'll be a habit that can be hard to break.When they don't listen to their thoughts, they can feel like they are doing something wrong. They tend to gain a lot of concern with stress. For example, with the hand washing, they would wash hands because of the fear of contamination.
